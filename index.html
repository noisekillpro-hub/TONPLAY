Game Rules

🔄

Swap tiles

Only horizontal and vertical swaps. Invalid swaps are automatically reversed.

⏱

60 seconds

Each session is 60 seconds. Score as many points as possible.

💣

Boosters

Hammer removes 1 tile. Bomb clears all tiles of a type. Shuffle mixes the board.

🧊

Locked tiles

Some tiles are frozen. Match tiles beside them to break the ice (2 hits required).

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We have answers.

Is the game free to play? ▾
Yes, Crypro Match 3 is completely free to download and play. You get 10 free game sessions every 6 hours. You can also watch an ad to earn +5 extra sessions.
Do I need to create an account? ▾
No registration required. The app uses anonymous authentication — your progress is saved automatically to a secure ID on our servers without any personal information.
How do I earn CRT tokens? ▾
Play a 60-second Match-3 session. Every 1000 points earns you 1 CRT token. The maximum reward per game is 15 CRT (requires scoring 12,000+ points).
What is the minimum withdrawal amount? ▾
The minimum withdrawal is 1 TON. Network fee is 0.15 TON per transaction. You need a valid TON blockchain wallet address to receive funds.
How long does withdrawal take? ▾
Withdrawal requests are processed manually within 24 hours. Once processed, you will see the funds in your TON wallet. Status updates are shown in the withdrawal history.
What is a TON wallet? ▾
TON is a blockchain network. To withdraw your CRT, you need a TON-compatible wallet such as Tonkeeper, TON Wallet, or MyTonWallet. These are free apps available on iOS and Android.
Why was my withdrawal rejected? ▾
Withdrawals may be rejected if: the wallet address is invalid, the amount is below minimum, or if suspicious activity is detected on the account. Contact support for details.
I lost my progress. What can I do? ▾
Since the app uses anonymous authentication, your progress is tied to the app installation. Reinstalling the app will create a new anonymous account. Contact support as soon as possible if this happens.
Are boosters free? ▾
New players start with 3 of each booster (Hammer, Bomb, Shuffle). Additional boosters can be purchased with CRT tokens in the in-game shop (coming soon).
