How can we help?

Find answers about gameplay, tokens, withdrawals, and account management.

1

Download the app

Install Crypro Match 3 from Google Play. The app is free to download.

2

Create an account

Sign up with your email. No crypto wallet required to start playing.

3

Play & earn CRT

Complete games to earn CRT tokens. Each session rewards you based on your score.

4

Withdraw to TON

Convert CRT to TON and withdraw to your wallet once you reach the minimum threshold.

The board

A 6×6 grid of coloured tiles. Swap two adjacent tiles to create a match of 3 or more identical tiles in a row or column.

Timer

Each game lasts 60 seconds. Make as many matches as possible before time runs out to maximize your score.

Boosters

Bomb (B), Shuffle (↺), and Hammer (H) — each starts with 3 charges per session. Use them wisely!

Daily limit

10 free games per day. Watch an ad to earn 5 extra games. The limit resets every 24 hours.

What is CRT?

CRT (Crypto Match Token) is the in-game currency you earn by playing. Convert it to TON and withdraw to your wallet.

Exchange rate

The CRT → TON rate updates periodically. Check the current rate on the Token screen inside the app.

Withdrawals

Connect a TON-compatible wallet (e.g. Tonkeeper). A minimum balance and network fee apply. Processed within 24 hours.

Security

Balance stored securely on Firebase. We never ask for your private key. Only connect wallets via the official in-app screen.

Why can't I swap tiles that look adjacent?
Only tiles directly next to each other (horizontally or vertically) can be swapped. Diagonal swaps are not allowed. If no match results, the swap is automatically reversed.
My CRT balance didn't update after a game.
Balance updates require an internet connection and may take a few seconds. Close and reopen the app. If the issue persists after 5 minutes, contact support.
How do I get more games after hitting the daily limit?
Tap "Watch Ad" on the Home screen to earn 5 extra games instantly. The daily limit resets every 24 hours.
Can I play without an internet connection?
No. An active connection is required to authenticate, save progress, and credit CRT rewards.
Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?
Yes. The exact threshold is shown on the Withdraw screen and depends on the current CRT → TON exchange rate.
